ABOUT US

Trip Navigator Corporate Travel solutions has been created with a vision to simplify Corporate Travel & Event management by integrating all MICE services & offering them online.

The portal gives you the opportunity to plan & execute your next initiative online in the most optimum manner by offering you various options to choose from should it be an offsite, sales incentive trip, annual meeting, Event, etc. A wide range of hotels across the country to choose from. With an excellent search engine to help you shortlist the hotel of you choice basis your requirement, budget & location of choice, and a chance to post & review client feedback to assist in making the right choices.

Merchandise options across the country for you to select, should it be reward options or takeaway gifts. Browse through various options & have a hassle free experience.

A wide range of Team building options in the destination of your choice through carefully selected partners who understand your need and offer you the best team building solutions. These could be outdoors or Indoor, training based or physical, motivational speakers & lots more to choose from.

Event solutions for your Gala dinners, Themed parties, Conference’s, etc, to ensure that you have all the options available at the most reasonable prices. A wide range Artist from dance troupes, MC’s, illusionists, local performances & stunning acts across the country, to choose from.

All this & much more at one place. The one stop shop for your Travel & Event planning.
